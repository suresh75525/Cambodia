About:

         The Kalaimagal Matriculation & Higher Secondary School was established in June 1986 by Sri Balaji Educstional Trust . The School is a progressive ,learner-focused school that values the individual and believes in nurturing self-esteem , confidence and independent thinking ,while also encouraging self-discipline and responsibility.

The School was started for the following ideals :

  • To Provide good standard of education through State Matriculation Syllabus.
  • To develop harmonious development among the students.
  • To develop Patriotsm , Religious Faith ,Good Citizenship qualities among the pupils.
  • To inculcate Good Discipline , Good Manners , Good Habits ,Give and Take Policy, Leadership qualities, Friendliness and Tolerance in the minds of the students.
  • We encourage excellence in academcs , extra curricular and co-curricular activities.

         The School possesses spacious well ventilated class rooms , modern teaching aids, well equipped Physics, Chemistry, Biology Labs with the latest Computer Systems and Internet connection in Computer Lab, Library facility ,Play Grounds, Indoor and Out-door games facilities, facilities for Yoga and Meditation, Audio visual room, Transport facility, Karate etc.

          The School has obtained recognition from the Govt.of Tamilnadu in the year 1989. Since the school has been recognized from First Standard onwards , the Children of the State and Central Govt. Employees can get fees reimbursement facilities. They can also get educational card pass and bus token pass facilities. The SC/ST students can get Tamlinadu State Government scholarship.

          The school has Managing Committee, Advisory Committee, Teachers Council, Academic Councilors and School Welfare Committee for the growth and development of the school.
